# TransitTeam Mobile — User Guide

The native companion app for courier companies running **TransitTeam** on WordPress.
Drivers receive and manage jobs in the field; dispatchers assign work and monitor the fleet.
Everything you see is stored on your device, so the app keeps working when you lose signal.

## The 60-second overview

1. **Install** the app on your phone (Android or iOS).
2. **Connect** it once to your company's TransitTeam site using a Site URL, Client ID, and
   Client Secret your administrator gives you.
3. **Sign in** with your normal TransitTeam username and password.
4. The app downloads your jobs to the phone. After that it **always shows your saved data
   instantly** and quietly syncs with the server in the background.
5. What you can do depends on your **role** (driver / dispatcher / administrator) and your
   company's **assignment mode** (Centralized or Decentralized) — see
   [05 — Views Reference](05-views-reference.md).

> **Roles in plain terms**
> - **Driver** — sees and updates their own jobs.
> - **Dispatcher / Administrator** — sees all jobs, all drivers, and all customers, and can
>   assign jobs to drivers.

> **Assignment modes in plain terms**
> - **Centralized** — dispatchers hand jobs out; drivers just work their assigned list.
> - **Decentralized** — drivers see an **Available** pool and **claim** jobs themselves.
