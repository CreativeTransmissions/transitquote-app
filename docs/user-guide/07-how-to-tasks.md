## Update a job's status
*Everyone.*

1. Open the job from your **Jobs** list.
2. Tap **Update status**.
3. Pick the new status from the list (your current one is ticked).
4. Tap **Confirm** in the prompt.

The job updates instantly and syncs in the background. If you're offline it shows **↻ Pending sync**
until it sends.

---

## Claim an available job *(decentralized drivers)*

1. On the **Jobs** screen, open the **Available** tab.
2. Tap the job you want.
3. Tap **Claim job**, then **Claim** to confirm.

The job moves to your **My Jobs** tab.

> If there's no **Available** tab, your team is in **Centralized** mode — a dispatcher assigns jobs
> instead. See below.

---

## Assign a job to a driver *(dispatchers)*

1. Open the job.
2. In the **Assignment** section, tap **Assign driver**.
3. Choose a driver from the picker.
4. Tap **Assign** to confirm.

> If an assignment comes back as **failed**, the server rejected it (for example, that driver isn't
> a valid target). Open the **Sync problems** sheet for the reason — see
> [Fix a change that failed](#fix-a-change-that-failed-to-save).

## Filter the jobs list
*Everyone (driver filter is dispatcher-only).*

1. On the **Jobs** screen, tap **Filter** (bottom-left).
2. Choose any combination of:
   - **Status** chips (tap to include several).
   - **Driver** *(dispatcher only)* — Any or a specific driver.
   - **Scheduled date** — **From** / **To** as `YYYY-MM-DD`.
3. Tap **Apply**.

The Filter button shows a number badge while filters are active. To remove them, tap **Filter →
Clear all**.

## Open a route or stop in Maps
*Everyone, on jobs that have stops.*

- **Whole route:** open the job → **Route** section → **Open route in Maps**.
- **A single stop:** open the job → **Route** section → tap the stop.

Your phone's Google Maps opens with directions. (There's no map *inside* the app by design — it
hands off to the maps app you already know.)

---

## Call or email a customer or driver
*Customer contact: everyone on a job. Driver contact: dispatchers.*

- **Customer:** open the job → **Customer** section → tap **Phone** to call or **Email** to email.
- **Driver:** *(dispatcher)* open **Drivers** → a driver → tap **Phone** or **Email**.

The phone's dialler or email app takes over.

---

## Find a customer's jobs *(dispatchers)*

1. From the **Jobs** screen header, tap **Customers**.
2. Use the **Search** box (name, email, or phone) or scroll.
3. Tap the customer.
4. Their **Job history** is listed; tap any job to open it.

## Work offline
*Everyone.*

You don't need to do anything special:

- The app shows your saved data immediately, with an **Offline** banner at the top.
- You can still **update statuses**, **claim**, and **assign** — changes queue as **pending**.
- When you regain signal, queued changes send automatically and fresh data pulls down.

See [06 — Sync & Offline](06-sync-and-offline.md) for what each indicator means.

## Fix a change that failed to save
*Everyone.*

1. Tap the **N failed** badge next to the "Jobs" title (or open the affected job and use its red
   banner).
2. In **Sync problems**, read the explanation for the change.
3. Choose:
   - **Retry** — if it was a temporary problem, or you've since corrected the cause.
   - **Discard** — to drop the change entirely.

## Switch between sites
*Everyone with more than one site saved.*

1. From the **Jobs** header, tap **Profile**.
2. Under **Switch site**, tap the site you want.
3. Confirm. The app reloads with that site's data.

To **add** a new site, see [04 — Connecting → Adding and switching sites](04-connecting.md#adding-and-switching-sites).

---

## Turn notifications on or off
*Everyone.*

The app asks once when you first sign in. To change your mind later, use your phone's settings:

- **Android:** Settings → Apps → **TQApp** → Notifications.
- **iOS:** Settings → Notifications → **TQApp**.

---

## Log out
*Everyone.*

1. From the **Jobs** header, tap **Profile**.
2. Tap **Log out** and confirm.

You'll return to the Sign in screen. Your saved data for that site is cleared on logout; signing
back in re-downloads it.
